Explore more about Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to support life. It has a unique atmosphere composed primarily of nitrogen and oxygen, which sustains diverse ecosystems and regulates temperature. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, making it the "Blue Planet." Earth has a dynamic climate system, with weather patterns influenced by its atmosphere, oceans, and geological activity. It has a strong magnetic field that protects it from harmful solar radiation. The planet orbits the Sun in about 365.25 days, with a tilted axis that creates seasonal variations.
